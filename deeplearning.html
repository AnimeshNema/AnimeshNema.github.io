Predicting grade of road for Autonomous Vehicles using Supervised Deep Learning

A novel deep learning approach towards estimating the grade of the road ahead of the vehicle through vision has been presented.The aim is to provide an insight into how a vehicle will change its power distribution if it has the knowledge about the upcoming grade. This will help in improving the fuel economy, ride safety and comfort to quite an extent.

IMU(Inertial Measurement Unit) and GPS(Global Positioning System) Data were utilized to label the images and the labeled images were fed to the Convolutional Neural Network. The model was trained on thousands of such images and tested on a real-time video impementation.

SOFTWARE USED: Python, Tensorflow, Keras, OpenCV
