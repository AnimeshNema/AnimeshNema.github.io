Adaptive Trajectory Control for a Robotic Arm Subject to Varying Load

Implemented adaptive trajectory controller on a 2-link robot arm subject to varying payloads. The goal was that the robot arm maintained its trajectory while carrying loads of uncertain and dynamic mass.

Two different appraches were executed and their performances were tested against each other. While the first imlementation was based on Linear Parameterization, the second approach used Function Aprroximation Techniques to avoid linear paramterization.

Graphs were plotted for the angles of the link and the error in estimated and true mass with respect to time.

SOFTWARE USED: MATLAB
